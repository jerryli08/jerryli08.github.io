Electric Vehicle

Overview

I created this robot individually for the 2024-25 Science Olympiad "Electric Vehicle" event. My partner helped with testing the vehicle.

CAD

Assembly & Testing

Integrated test of BLDC motor and MT6701 Rotary Absolute Magnetic Encoder.

Arduino Nano outputs PWM for BLDC ESC, and reads MT6701 through I2C.

First ground test with HDF (hardboard) CNC plates.

Plates were too heavy and ESC was configured unoptimally in BLHeli_32 Suite, hence poor performance.

The electric vehicle mid-rebuilt, with redesigned 1.5mm G10 fiberglass CNC plates.

Vehicle had significantly reduced weight, and was significantly faster.
