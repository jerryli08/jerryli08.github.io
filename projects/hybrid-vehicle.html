Overview

At the MIT Beaver Works Summer Institute, my group developed a hybrid aerial-ground vehicle system.
The aerial vehicle (UAV) has custom landing gear, which docks with actuated latches on the ground vehicle (UGV).
This allows each individual agent to carry the other: the UGV carries the UAV to save energy, and the UAV carries the UGV for flying over impassable terrain or for tasks that require aerial perspectives.
After the Institute, we conducted more tests, collected more data, and created a research poster. The poster accepted for presentation at the 2025 MIT Undergraduate Research Technology Conference (10/10 - 10/12).
